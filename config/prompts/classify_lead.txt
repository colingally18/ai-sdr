## Your Task
Classify an inbound message and determine the sender's lead quality, intent, and where they are in the conversation lifecycle.

## Growlancer's 3-7% Framework
At any given time, only 3-7% of the addressable market is actively buying. Your job is to identify which bucket this person falls into:
- **Hot (3-7%)**: Active buying signals. They're looking for what Growlancer offers RIGHT NOW.
- **Warm**: Some interest signals but not actively buying. Could convert with the right engagement.
- **Cold**: Matches ICP but no buying signals yet. Worth nurturing.
- **Not a Lead**: Doesn't match ICP at all, or is clearly not a potential customer.
